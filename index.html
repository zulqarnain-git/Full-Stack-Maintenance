How It Works

1

Admin Sets Up

Uploads contracts, defines budgets, assigns representatives.

2

Client Submits Request

Fills out a maintenance form with vehicle and part details.

3

Admin Reviews

Approves or rejects the request, forwards to a representative.

4

Representative Acts

Delivers parts, supervises installation, uploads documents.

5

System Notifies

WhatsApp alerts keep everyone informed at each stage.
